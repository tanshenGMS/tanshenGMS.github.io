Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Junie 21, 2025

"Victory! SteelSquad" is a free-to-download, single-player offline game developed and provided by me, where players participate in competitive matches between two AI-driven factions.

I attach great importance to user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I handle and use your information when you use this application.

This Privacy Policy aims to comply with applicable data protection and privacy laws and regulations, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), and other relevant laws in regions where I operate or where this application is accessible.

1. Personal Information Not Directly Collected

This application is an offline, standalone game that does not require account registration. It does not ask you to create a user account, nor does it require you to input your real name, email address, phone number, mailing address, or other personally identifiable information. It also does not collect personal information directly linked to your real identity.

2. Automatically Collected Information

When you use this application, the integrated third-party service, Google AdMob SDK, may automatically collect the following non-personal information, or information considered personal information in certain regions, for the purposes of improving ad performance, analytics, and fraud prevention. The collection and use of this data strictly adhere to the following platform-specific requirements and your authorization:

For Android Devices:

  • IP Address: Collects the device's IP address (the user's or device's approximate location, covering an area greater than or equal to 3 square kilometers, such as the user's city or a location that Android's ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION permission might provide). This information is primarily used to estimate the device's general location for ad targeting and fraud prevention.
  • User Interaction with Products: Collects information about user interactions with products, including app launches, clicks, and video views. This interaction information can be used to improve ad performance.
  • Diagnostic Information: Collects information related to the performance of the app and the SDK, including crash logs, app launch times, hang rates, and power consumption. This diagnostic information can also be used for advertising and analytics.
  • Device Identifiers: Collects the device's Android Advertising ID (AAID) and App Set ID. These identifiers are used for ad serving, frequency capping, conversion tracking, and ad personalization.
  • Ad Data: For example, ads viewed by the user, which supports analytics and advertising features.
  • Performance Data: Collects user-related performance data, such as app launch times, hang rates, or power consumption, which can be used to evaluate user behavior, understand the effectiveness of existing product features, and plan new features. Performance data may also be used for displaying ads, including sharing with other entities that display ads.

For iOS Devices:

  • Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA): With your authorization, collects and uses the device's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA). This identifier is used for ad serving, frequency capping, conversion tracking, and ad personalization. Users can manage their tracking preferences through iOS device settings or in-app privacy settings.
  • IP Address: Collects the device's IP address, used to estimate the device's approximate location (e.g., the user's city), primarily for ad targeting and fraud prevention.
  • User Interaction with Products: Collects information about user interactions with products, including app launches, clicks, and video views. This interaction information can be used to improve ad performance.
  • Diagnostic Information: Collects information related to the performance of the app and the SDK, including crash logs, app launch times, hang rates, and power consumption. This diagnostic information can also be used for advertising and analytics.
  • Performance Data: Collects user-related performance data, such as app launch times, hang rates, or power consumption, which can be used to evaluate user behavior, understand the effectiveness of existing product features, and plan new features. Performance data may also be used for displaying ads, including sharing with other entities that display ads.
  • Ad Data: For example, ads viewed by the user, which supports analytics and advertising features.

Even if you choose not to receive personalized ads, ad platforms may still use some device information (such as IP address, device type, rough geo-location, etc.) to display non-personalized ads unrelated to you, in order to ensure the basic functionality of ad display.

All user data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted during transmission using Transport Layer Security (TLS). The collection of this information follows user authorization preferences and is entirely managed and processed by third-party services in accordance with their privacy policies.

I strictly comply with the terms regarding the use of advertising identifiers set by relevant platforms (Google Play and Apple App Store) and respect your privacy choices.

3. Third-Party Services and Their Privacy Policies

To provide advertising services in this application, I integrate the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob: AdMob automatically collects the data described in Section 2 of this policy to display ads to you (including personalized and non-personalized ads, as well as rewarded ads). This data is shared with Google. Google will use this data according to its own privacy policy. I strongly recommend that you review Google's privacy policy to understand how Google handles your information: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Unity In-App Purchasing (Unity IAP): This application integrates Unity IAP to support in-app purchases via Google Play and the App Store. When users make a purchase, the result is only stored locally to determine entitlement activation. No information that may be considered personal or private is stored or actively shared with any third parties other than Unity, Google, or Apple. Unity Analytics is not enabled in this application, so no user data is collected or analyzed.You can review Unity's privacy policy here:https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

I use the information automatically collected through third-party services primarily for the following purposes:

  • Provide in-app advertising services: Including loading and displaying interstitial and rewarded ads, and enabling the function of watching rewarded ads to obtain in-game rewards; if your consent is obtained, personalized recommended ads that better align with your preferences and interests may also be provided based on your device information, IP address, usage data, etc.
  • Analyze ad performance: Understand the effectiveness of ad displays and user interactions.
  • Prevent fraud and inappropriate activities: Detect and block invalid ad traffic or fraudulent behavior.

6. Legal Basis for Processing Your Information (Applicable only to EEA and UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom (UK), the legal basis for processing your personal information depends on the related processing activity and its purpose. For data processed by AdMob for advertising purposes:

  • Based on Your Consent: Especially when it comes to personalized advertising, I rely on your explicit consent to process your data. You can withdraw your consent through the in-game privacy settings.
  • Based on My Legitimate Interests: For example, to display non-personalized ads, perform basic ad performance analysis, and prevent fraud, which are necessary for me to provide free game services, and I believe these interests are legitimate while fully safeguarding your rights and freedoms.

When the application is launched for the first time within the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom (UK), Google's User Messaging Platform SDK (UMP SDK) will display a Consent Management Platform (CMP) interface to obtain your authorization for personalized ads and related data usage. You can also modify your consent preferences at any time in the game settings.

9. Protection of Minors

This application features a retro, nostalgic animation style and is not intended for minors.

According to store questionnaires, the content rating on Google Play is 16+. However, I have manually set the download restriction on Google Play to adults aged 18 and above. Therefore, although the content rating on Google Play is 16+, the download access is restricted to adults (18+). Additionally, if Google determines a user is underage, access to the application will be restricted accordingly.

This means the application is not directed at any underage users and does not actively collect or process any personal information from minors. Reasonable measures have been taken to protect the privacy of minors, and the restrictions of this application have been developed with reference to applicable regulations, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Since this application has taken all reasonable steps to avoid obtaining or determining users’ age, it is therefore unable to directly identify or verify a user’s age. If parents discover that their underage child may have downloaded and used this application, they may take action through the relevant mechanisms provided by the App Store or Google Play. Within the limits of applicable law and my capabilities, I will provide necessary assistance and guidance.

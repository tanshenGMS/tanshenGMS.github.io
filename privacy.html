Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Super Armored League is a free single-player offline game we developed, where players compete against AI-driven opponents in a non-combat competitive experience.

This policy explains how we handle data when you use our app, especially regarding personalized advertising under your consent.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or address.

However, with your explicit consent, our app may collect certain non-personal information through integrated third-party services for the purpose of personalized advertising, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • Ad viewing and interaction behavior
  • Crash reports and performance analytics

This data is collected only if you agree, and is managed solely by the third-party services according to their respective privacy policies.

2. Third-party Services and Their Policies

We use the following third-party services to provide ads and analytics:

  • Google AdMob
  • Unity

You can manage whether to allow personalized ads through the privacy settings page inside the app.

3. Ad Types

Our app includes the following ad types:

  • Interstitial Ads
  • Rewarded Ads

The ad content is provided by Google AdMob based on your consent.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If a parent or guardian believes that data has been collected from a child, they may modify consent preferences via the in-app privacy settings, or uninstall the app to discontinue data sharing.

5. Data Storage and Security

We do not store user data on our own servers. All data collection and storage is handled by the third-party SDKs listed above.
